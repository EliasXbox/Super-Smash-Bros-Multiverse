# Super Smash Bros: Multiverse

A fan-made platform fighting game inspired by the iconic Super Smash Bros series, delivering a complete, personalized, and action-packed experience!

Get ready for electrifying battles with your favorite fighters, unique stages, and various game modes—all set in a dynamic and fluid 2D environment.

# Key Features:

Diverse Characters: A growing roster of fighters, including original and reimagined classics.

Varied Game Modes: Classic Mode, All-Star, local multiplayer, and more.

Custom Stages: Unique arenas with original music and design for strategic battles.

Refined Combat System: Fighting mechanics capturing the essence of Super Smash Bros, with attacks, dodges, double jumps, and more.

Active Community: Regular updates with new content, characters, and tweaks, driven by fan feedback.


Join the fight and prove your skills in the world of Super Smash Bros: Multiverse!
